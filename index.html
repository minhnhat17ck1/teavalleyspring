Welcome to the Valley

Nestled in the heart of Spring, TX, Tea Valley is your local sanctuary for authentic bubble tea and creative refreshments. We're passionate about quality, using only the freshest ingredients to craft unique flavors that will brighten your day.

From our signature milk teas to our vibrant fruit refreshers and savory snacks, there's a taste of happiness waiting for everyone. Come in, relax, and find your favorite flavor!
